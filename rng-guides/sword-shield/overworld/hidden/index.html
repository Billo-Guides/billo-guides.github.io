Sword/Shield Hidden RNG

If you made it this far, it must mean you have an interest in RNG abusing a Pokemon from a hidden spawn! This page will cover the whole process, from finding a desirable location to achieving a final result.

Getting Started:

Before starting, do the following:
  • Find an ideal location and weather condition for your Pokemon using the SWSH Encounter-slots finder
  • Once found, head to the location in-game and find our target Pokemon.
  • Run far enough away so the Pokemon is despawned, save, then reboot the game to the overworld.
  • Press the Home button on your controller, click connect on the GUI, then fill in the details of your hunt on the GUI accordingly.

Time to get to work!

We will be aiming for a "Pokemon" in "Location", and our goal will be to spawn in the shaky grass on the calculated target frame, given to us by the RNG tool GUI. We will be using the advancement methods listed in the main page of this guide, be sure to check out that page if you have not already done so.
