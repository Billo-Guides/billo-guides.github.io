Spawner Legendary RNG (Retail)

This page will provide in-depth information about how to RNG abuse Legendary Pokemon in Legends: Arceus that are tied to what is known as Spawners. We will be using RNG automation programs written by Lusamine, as mentioned on the main page.

From here it is assumed that you have already followed all of the setup & installation instructions found on the introductory page of this guide.

About Spawner RNG:

Pokemon that are tied to spawners have their seed generated when you enter the map or cave that they are located in. The seed remains the same until you defeat the Pokemon and have it respawn. Respawning will be a consistent +1 advancement to the Spawner seed.

Leaving the area and heading back to Jubilife HQ will refresh the spawner seed of every Pokemon on the map, including Legendary Pokemon. We can use the Spawner Seed Solver and Spawner RNG Calculator scripts to find and check our current seed for desirable results.

It is recommended to have a strong Pokemon, lots of healing items (e.g Elixirs, Potions, Revives) and lots of throwable items (Ball of Mud, Sticky Glob etc.) before getting started. You will need enough resources to consistently defeat/stun the Legendary Pokemon that you are hunting.


Now we know the basics about Spawner-based encounters, it's time to get to work!

Getting To Work!

After gathering resources, disable autosave in the in-game settings to avoid catastrophe. Checking your seed requires capturing the Pokemon and returning to Jubilife HQ, which will force the game to save with a caught legendary unless autosave is disabled.

Finding Your Current Seed & Frame Advancements:

In order to find your current seed you will need access to a console that is running Custom Firmware, this most commonly used method is dumping your Pokemon using somebody elses SysBot. Many free SysBots can be found in public Discord servers, you can find a list of servers that host SysBots with a quick Google search.

How to find your current seed:

Sysbot.NET is capable to dump any Pokemon you shown inside the trade window without needing to physically trade the Pokemon (as this would force a save and lock you into the same seed permanently). This gives retail players the ability to check their seed and reset the game if the seed does not produce any desirable results.

  1. Head into the map location of your desired Legendary, then manually save the game.
  2. Capture the legendary, then return to Jubilife without saving the game.
  3. Initiate a Sysbot dump trade, then dump the Legendary Pokemon that you caught.
  4. Reset the game, this time, KO the Pokemon instead of catching it.
  5. Advance a single frame, then capture the next Legendary that spawns.
  6. Initiate another Sysbot trade, dump the Pokemon again and reset the game.
  7. You should now have two sequencial pa8 files and be back in correct map location.
  8. Put both of the dumped pa8 files inside the pa8s folder that was created earlier.
  9. Run the Seed Solver script until a seed is produced.

How to advance the RNG state:

Frame advancement methods will differ depending on if your Pokemon is an forced capture or despawns when defeated, this section will cover how to advance frames in both of these scenarios.

Despawns When Defeated:

These include - Tornadus, Thundurus, Landorus, Enamorus, Shaymin & Darkrai.

  1. Knockout the legendary.
  2. Fly to a campsite, then sleep until night, then sleep again until evening.
  3. Fly to a cave and enter, then save and reboot the game.
  4. Fly back to the legendary and wait 1 weather cycle for the legendary to respawn.
  5. Repeat from step 1 until the desired frame has been hit.

While not required, it is recommended to sleep until evening after step 3, this will guarantee a weather cycle to occur within 3 minutes, otherwise you may be waiting longer than necessary for one to occur.

Note - Tornadus or Thundurus will only spawn in specific weather conditions, Tornadus will only respawn in a blizzard, and Thundurus will only respawn in a thunderstorm.

Forced Capture:

These include - Uxie, Mesprit, Azelf, Heatran & Cresselia.

  1. Knockout the legendary.
  2. Click through the menus until you regain control of your character.
  3. Repeat from step 1 until the desired frame has been hit.

These Pokemon are tied to main story quests, which means there is no wait time between encounters whatsoever.

Note - All of these Pokemon are located inside caves except Cresselia, which means you cannot leave the cave once you are already inside, come well prepared!

Unique Cases:

These include - Manaphy & Phione.

  1. Knockout the legendary.
  2. Fly to a campsite, then sleep until night, then sleep again until evening.
  3. Warp to Fire Pit Island and enter the cave, then save and reboot the game.
  4. Fly back to the cave that contains Manaphy and wait 1 weather cycle for it to respawn.
  5. Enter the cave, check to see if your Pokemon has respawned.
  6. Repeat from step 1 until the desired frame has been hit.
Manaphy & Phione are unique cases in that it does not immediately respawned but is contained inside a cave, so the advancement method is more akin to the method for Overworld legendaries.

After finding your current seed you can paste it into the Spawner RNG Calculation script and search for desirable results. If you do not find desirable results you will need to leave the area back to Jubilife HQ then start again from seed checking step 1.

After a short wait you should now see that a group seed has been produced, copy it, this seed is the seed that the Calculation Script will need to calculate the upcoming advancements of the Pokemon on the spawner that you are searching.

Copy the group seed that was produced by the Seed Solver Script, then paste it into the Spawner RNG Calculation Script on the line that starts with ulong group_seed = 0x (located towards the top of the script).

ulong init = unchecked(group_seed)
Xoroshiro128Plus rng = new(init)

Figure 3 - Setting Filters - Calculation Script



To search for flawless IV spreads, activate the filters found towards the bottom of the script. By default the IV filters will be disabled and will need to be activated manually, this can be done by removing the comments (*/) that surround the IV filters at the top and the bottom.

For Heatran specifically, you will also need to set the GenderCompare value at the top of the script to 127, Heatran is the only case where this value should be changed from -1 as the rest of these legendaries have a fixed gender or are genderless.

If your search result is not desirable or you do not find any results when searching with filters, you will need to return to Jubilife then back to the map that contains the legendary you are hunting in order to refresh the seed, then find your current seed again (see Figure 1).
