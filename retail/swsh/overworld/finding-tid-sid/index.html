Finding your TID/SID

This tutorial will provide coverage of how to find your 5 digit TID/SID so that you can RNG abuse overworld encounters to the fullest extent. Your 5 digit TID/SID are values that determine if a wild Pokémon will be shiny, so it's important that we know what these values are before starting!

In Sword/Shield you cannot RNG abuse your TID/SID, this tutorial is only for finding your TID/SID in an existing save file.

Getting Started:

There are two methods you can use to find your TID/SID combination, both with a unique set of requirements. Your options are:
  1. Ask a friend or use a Discord bot running Sysbot.NET to dump the file, then view the data in PKHeX to find your TID/SID. (Recommended - NSO required)
  2. Find a shiny Pokémon without RNG abuse, identify your current seed after capture and then perform a reverse search using the generator to find your TID/SID. (No NSO required - Requires a shiny)

Sysbot.NET or the help of a friend:

Sysbot.NET or a friend running CFW can dump the data file of your Pokemon which you can then view in a PKHeX to easily find your TID/SID combination. Many Discord servers host their own Sysbots that users can use to dump the data of their Pokémon, Use Google to find one that you can use!

Requirements:
  • PKHeX - For viewing Pokémon data.
  • Nintendo Switch Online - For interacting with a friend or bot using Wi-Fi.
Finding your TID/SID with PKHeX (step-by-step tutorial):
  1. Download and extract the PKHeX.exe into an empty folder and run the program.
  3. Click Options at the top of the window, then click Settings, change the Blank Save Version to Sword or Shield.
  4. Dump the data of any Pokémon that you have caught on your save file with the help of a friend or Sysbot.
  5. Download the file and open it in PKHeX (right click the Pokémon + click view if you dragged it into a box slot).
  6. Head over to the OT/Misc tab, you should see the trainer data of your Pokémon on-screen.
  7. Hover over the TID/SID fields with your mouse, your 5 digit TID/SID will be displayed.
