Overworld Symbol Encounters

Worked examples for manipulating Symbol encounters in any weather condition.

The species that appear in random Symbol spawners are determined by a value called an "encounter slot", which ranges from 0 to 99. This value dictates which Pokémon from the encounter table will spawn. The available Pokémon in the encounter table remain the same until the weather in the area changes, which occurs at midnight (00:00). Be mindful of the current system time to prevent unexpected spawns due to incorrect weather conditions.

Important - From here, we assume that you are familiar with key concepts described on the main page. If you are unsure about seed finding, advancement methods, or NPC calibration, please review the relevant sections before continuing.
Figure 1 - RNG Tool Setup

Getting Started (Tool Setup):
Required Setup:
  1. In the main window, enter your TID/SID, select your game version, and check the Shiny/Mark Charm checkboxes if these items are present in your save file.

  2. Click Encounter Lookup in the top toolbar to open a sub-window. Enter the desired species name in the search field and select a SYMBOL encounter.

  3. Close the Encounter Lookup window, then input the relevant Location, Weather, and Species information into the Encounter Settings section.

  4. Enter the KO count for the Pokémon you are hunting into the KOs field. This ensures accurate prediction of extra rolls from Brilliant Aura boosts.

  5. Adjust your search range using the + field in Encounter Settings, then configure additional search filters such as Shiny, Marks, IVs, Brilliant Aura, or Height.
KO Mechanic & Brilliant Aura:

Pokémon with Brilliant Aura have increased Shiny odds and two guaranteed perfect IVs, making them ideal for high IV Shiny hunting. These boosts depend on how many of that species that you have knocked out, which can be checked by viewing the Pokédex entry.

To maximize Brilliant Aura boosts, 500 KOs on the target species are required. A practical alternative is 100 KOs with Shiny Charm boosts, improving Shiny odds from 1/1024 to 1/683.08. This provides a significant advantage with less effort. For more details on Brilliant Aura and Shiny rates, refer to Bulbapedia.

KO COUNT MUST BE CONFIGURED PROPERLY IN OWOOW TO AVOID POTENTIAL ISSUES.


Figure 2 - Game Setup

Game Setup:
  1. Remove any Pokémon currently stored in the {% include tooltips/daycare.html %} then fly to the required location and run around until you have found the spawner that contains your desired Pokémon.

  2. Move far enough away to completely despawn your target Pokémon, set the required weather condition, then save the game. Position yourself as close to the Pokémon's spawn border as possible without actually spawning it in the overworld.

  3. If you cannot find an optimal position, you can knock out any additional static spawners blocking your path. These will remain despawned as long as you do not move too far away or reload the area.

  4. Once positioned correctly, add your desired lead Pokémon to your party and save the game again. For detailed information on lead abilities and their effects, click the button below.

To confirm ideal positioning, look for a dust cloud or puddle beneath the Pokémon when it spawns. If these effects are not visible, you are standing too close to the spawner. (Note: This may be difficult to see if your target Pokémon is in tall grass or flying.)
