Advancing to the Target

It is recommended to target clusters where possible as this will further increase the chances of being able to hit your target frame.

Step-by-Step:
  1. Ensure the Consider Menu Close? checkbox is checked and your NPC count is entered correctly, search for your target again if needed.

  2. Advance toward your target using any of the methods listed on this page. Consider using wet weather or date skipping to cover large distances efficiently.

  3. Re-identify your seed periodically to avoid overshooting. Stop a few thousand frames early and position yourself correctly outside the spawn point.

  4. Use animations or closing the pause menu to continue advancing until you're approximately 100 frames from your target. Re-identify your seed once more.

  5. Search for your target again, then use animations to reach the exact frame. Each L3 button input on the party list advances the RNG state by +1 (100 inputs = 100 adv).

  6. Unpause the game. If you are using the Holding Direction? checkbox, hold a direction on the control stick when unpausing, otherwise, DO NOT hold a direction.

  7. Quickly run toward the target spawner to spawn the Pokemon in the overworld. Encounter the Pokemon and verify the results match what is expected.
