Seed Calibration

Learn how to use the Retail Seed Finder to find and monitor seeds.

This page contains information about finding and monitoring your current seed. Here you will learn the techniques used to find your initial seed and how to find your current RNG state at any given point.

Figure 1 - Retail Seedfinder Overview

Retail SeedFinder Overview:

On the far right of the main window, locate the button for the Retail Seed Finder, clicking it will open the sub-window displayed in Figure 1. This is where we will record a series of actions known as "motions" or "animations" to identify our current seed.

What are motions/animations?

When on the summary screen of a Pokémon, pressing down on the Left/Right Stick will cause the Pokémon to perform an animation. Most Pokémon have two seperate animations, which can be referred to as "Physical" or "Special". this gif provides a short demonstration on how to identify animation differences.

  • When Espeon lunges at the screen, we can identify that this is a physical animation.
  • When Espeon stands in place, we can identify that this is a special animation.

It is important to pick a Pokémon that has a clear animation difference to make the process as simple as possible. Espeon, Gallade and Drilbur are the most commonly used Pokémon for this process.


Figure 2 - Recording Animations

Recording Animations:

To determine your seed, you will need to accurately record 128 animations on the Pokémon summary screen, avoid using the party list for this process. Refer to Figure 2 for an example of proper practice.

Step-by-step:
  1. Open the pause menu, select a Pokémon and then click Summary, as stated, ensure that you are not using the party list.
  2. Make your Pokémon animate by pressing down on the Left/Right Stick, take note of if the animation was physical or special.
  3. If the animation type was physical, type or click 0 into the Animations field of the SeedFinder.
  4. If the animation type was special, type or click 1 into the Animations field of the SeedFinder.
  5. Repeat this process 128 times to get your seed, then click Update Main Form to paste your seed into the main window.

If you do not want to use 0's and 1's for seed identification, owoow also accepts , or s for special inputs, and . or p for physical inputs, use whichever input is most comfortable for you.


Figure 3 - Seed Found!

Confirming Your Seed:

After finding your seed and updating the main window, you will want to confirm that the seed is correct, this can be done in various different ways:

Re-identification (recommended):
  1. After identifying your seed, click Update Main Form in the Seed Finder to transfer the seed to the main window.
  2. Set the encounter type to Static in the Encounter Settings, and adjust the search filters to look for shiny Pokémon.
  3. If no results appear, increase the search range by modifying the + field in the Encounter Settings column, then search again until a result is found.
  4. Go to the Retail Tools section on the far-right of the owoow main window, and enter a small value (~50) into the + field.
  5. Click the Generate button and record animations in the Anim.: field until a seed is generated (this usually requires up to 20 animations).
  6. Once a seed is produced, click Update Seeds to populate the appropriate fields in the main window, and search for your shiny target again.
  7. If done correctly, the same shiny Pokémon should appear in the results both before and after re-identifying your seed. If not, your initial seed identification was likely incorrect. Repeat the process and try again.

Using the search table:
  1. Click Update Main Form after identifying your seed using the Seed Finder to update the main window.
  2. Select Static within the encounter settings, then change the search range (the + field) to a small number (~50 is fine).
  3. You will see that the next 50 search results have been produced, in this case, we will need to look inside the Animations column of the table.
  4. With the summary screen open, perform some extra animations to confirm that the animation aligns with the first result in the table.

Important - When re-identifying your seed after advancing the RNG state, you will need to set the value inside the + field of the Retail Tools section to a value greater than the number of advances you believe have occured. For example, if you believe that roughly 50000 advances have occured and you now wish to re-identify your seed, make sure the value inside the + field is set to a number that is > 50000 to avoid matching with an incorrect animation pattern.

Great! You should now have a basic understanding of identify and monitor your current seed. Ensure that you have a solid understanding of how to apply these concepts before continuing (you can leave this page open for future reference).
