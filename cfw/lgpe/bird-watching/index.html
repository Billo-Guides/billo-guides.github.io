LGPE Overworld Legendary Bird Hunting (CFW)

This guide will cover the process of how you can find members of the legendary bird trio Articuno, Zapdos & Moltres in the overworld easily, by using the moarencounterbots branch of the Sysbot.NET repository by Lusamine.

This guide assumes you are using a CFW Switch console running Atmosphere.

Getting Started:

You will need:
  • Sysbot.NET - Moarencounterbots - Latest commit
  • sys-botbase - Latest release
Visit the official Sysbot.NET Wiki for instructions on building the application using Visual Studio.

Installation Instructions:
  1. Download sys-botbase and extract the contents into an empty folder.
  2. Place the atmosphere folder from the sys-botbase download onto the root of your Switch SD card.
  3. Check the /atmosphere/contents directory of your SD card a folder named 430000000000000B.
  4. Reboot your Switch and launch CFW.

Bot Setup:

In order to use the LGPE encounterbots, open the .exe once and close it to generate a config.json. Right click this file and open it in a text editor and change the Mode to 100.

Figure 1 - Hub tab overview


The Hub tab is where we decide the type of encounter we are doing and which Pokemon to hunt for. Scroll down to EncounterLGPE and set your conditions, click on each of the fields and you will be met with a short description about them at the bottom of the window.

Now scroll down to StopConditions and set your filters for your legendary bird of choice, you can be as specific with the filters as you like, such as filtering for specific IVs or shiny type, or have the bot continue the routine if the legendary bird is not shiny.

Suggested settings and a detailed overview of each setting can be found on the moarencounterbots Wiki, it's recommended to read this page in its entirety before starting, it contains a lot of useful information!

Figure 2 - Log tab overview


The Log tab is where we monitor what is going on in-game, it shows us what the bot is doing in real-time and will contain all the information you need to know, such as when a legendary bird has spawned in the overworld, or if it's a shiny variant.

Figure 3 - Bots tab overview


The Bots tab is where we add bots, select the EncBotBirdWatch option in the dropdown menu, type your switch IP into the field and click Add to add a new instance of EncBotBirdWatch.

With setup out of the way, it's time hunt some legendary birds!

Let's go to work!

The goal is to spawn the legendary bird of choice in the overworld. we will use the bot to predict when a bird will spawn and to advance towards our target. It will then output the result and pause the game using the home button so that we can catch it!

- If you do not want the bot to stop on non-shiny results set the StopOnAllBirdsLGPE setting to FALSE in StopConditions
- If you wish to hunt for a specific bird you must set the StopOnSpecies setting to your bird of choice in StopConditions
- The bot assumes you are using the Pokecenter at Mt. Moon and have defeated/caught the legendary bird trio in their original static locations.
- The legendary birds are sky spawns, meaning you must have unlocked the ability to encounter sky spawns in-game, given shortly after defeating the Elite 4.

Shiny Hunting Stratergy:

To get optimal chances of finding a shiny legendary bird you should capture every bird that spawns of your chosen species. Upon capturing a Pokemon your shiny odds will increase for the next Pokemon that spawns of the same species.

For this reason it's recommended to have StopOnAllBirdsLGPE set to TRUE, you can then chain capture your chosen species for boosted shiny odds.

In this example we will be looking for any legendary bird at Mt. Moon Pokecenter, you can be more specific with the filters if you wish.

Figure 4 - Game Setup


Once you are stood outside Mt. Moon and have your bot configured properly, click Start All and head over to the Logs tab.

Figure 5 - Bot Started


The bot should now begin hunting for birds by exiting the Pokecenter then checking your RNG states. If you are having trouble exiting/entering the Pokecenter consider adjusting your timing settings.

Figure 6 - Target Found!


The bot will now begin waiting for your bird to spawn in the overworld, once it detects the encounter it will pause the game if it meets your stop conditions, otherwise it will continue looking for results.

Figure 7/8 - Result Found!



We found a Zapdos in the overworld! Time to catch it to boost the shiny odds of the next Zapdos we find.

Figure 9 - Result captured!


You can now repeat this process and farm as many birds as you desire!

Disclaimer: - This method does not guarantee shiny results, you may need to run the bot for a long period of time to find shinies.
