Target Found:

In the above example, a Shiny, Rare Mark Duraludon can be found in a cluster of advances from 26,000-26003, a cluster is a group of frames that produce the same end result. It is recommended to target clusters where possible as this will further increase the chances of being able to hit your target frame.

Undesirable Result?
  1. Close and re-open the game, then enter the overworld.
  2. Disconnect and reconnect from the RNG tool.
  3. Search for a new target frame until a desired result is found.
  4. Alternatively, use owoows Reset For Seed feature to automate the reset process.

Figure 4 - Settings Toggles

Settings Toggles:
Once you have calibrated your NPC count, do the following:
  1. Enter your NPC count into the NPCs field found in the Encounter Settings column (DO NOT USE THE ADVANCED SETTINGS NPC BOX, THIS IS ONLY USED FOR ROAMING ENCOUNTERS!).

  2. Click the Consider Menu Close? checkbox if you have not already done so, then search for your target again. This will give you the frame that you are expected to close the pause menu on.

  3. If you can see your target frame after searching again, great! You do not need to do anything else. If you do not see your target frame, select the Holding Direction? checkbox and search again.

  4. There is a very high chance of being able to hit your target frame with menu close prediction, either with or without holding a direction, if you cannot hit your target frame either way, it is recommended to search for a new target.

Figure 5 - Advancing To The Target

Advancing To The Target:
Step-by-step:
  1. Use any of the advancement methods found on this page to advance as close to your target frame as possible.

  2. If you have moved location, leave yourself ample time to re-position yourself next to your target Pokemon, pause the game when you are a few hundred frames away from the target.

  3. Disconnect and reconnect to owoow, this will start your advances from 0. Search for your target again after doing so, then use animation advances to reach the new target frame.

  4. Unpause the game. If you are using the Holding Direction? checkbox, ensure you are holding a direction on your control stick when unpausing, otherwise, ensure you are NOT holding a direction.

  5. Quickly run towards the spawner of your desired Pokemon, if done correctly, your Pokemon should have been spawned on the target frame, encounter it and check if it matches what is expected.
