Overworld Fishing Encounters

Worked examples for manipulating Fishing encounters in any weather condition.

The species that appear in fishing pools are determined by a value called an "encounter slot", which ranges from 0 to 99. This value dictates which Pokémon from the encounter table will spawn. The available Pokémon in the encounter table remain the same until the weather in the area changes, which occurs at midnight (00:00). Be mindful of the current system time to prevent unexpected spawns due to incorrect weather conditions.

Important - From here, we assume that you are familiar with key concepts described on the main page. If you are unsure about seed monitoring, advancement methods, or NPC calibration, please review the relevant sections before continuing.
Figure 1 - RNG Tool Setup

Getting Started (Tool Setup):
Required Setup:
  1. Enter the IP address of your console in the main window and click Connect. This will automatically read your current seed and save file details.

  2. Click Encounter Lookup in the top toolbar to open a sub-window. Enter the desired species name in the search field and select a FISHING encounter.

  3. Close the Encounter Lookup window, then input the relevant Location, Weather, and Species information into the Encounter Settings section.

  4. Enter the KO count for the Pokémon you are hunting into the KOs field. This ensures accurate prediction of extra rolls from Brilliant Aura boosts.

  5. Adjust your search range using the + field in Encounter Settings, then configure additional search filters such as Shiny, Marks, IVs, Brilliant Aura, or Height.
KO Mechanic & Brilliant Aura:

Pokémon with Brilliant Aura have increased Shiny odds and two guaranteed perfect IVs, making them ideal for high IV Shiny hunting. These boosts depend on how many of that species that you have knocked out, which can be checked by viewing the Pokédex entry.

To maximize Brilliant Aura boosts, 500 KOs on the target species are required. A practical alternative is 100 KOs with Shiny Charm boosts, improving Shiny odds from 1/1024 to 1/683.08. This provides a significant advantage with less effort. For more details on Brilliant Aura and Shiny rates, refer to Bulbapedia.

KO COUNT MUST BE CONFIGURED PROPERLY IN OWOOW TO AVOID POTENTIAL ISSUES.


Figure 2 - Game Setup

Game Setup:
  1. Remove any Pokémon currently stored in the {% include tooltips/daycare.html %}, then fly to the required location and locate a fishing pool. All fishing pools in the area will contain the same selection of Pokémon.

  2. Move far enough away to completely despawn the fishing pool, set the required weather condition, then save the game. If the fishing pool respawns you are standing too close. Despawn the fishing pool by moving further back.

  3. If you cannot find an ideal position, knock out any static spawners blocking your path. These will remain despawned as long as you do not move too far away or reload the area.

  4. Once correctly positioned, add your desired lead Pokémon to your party and save the game again. For detailed information on lead abilities and their effects, click the button below.

Fishing pools will not spawn while the player is riding a bike. To ensure correct positioning, dismount your bike and move far enough away so that the fishing pool does not appear, do not mount your bike again after confirming that your positioning is correct.
