Settings Toggles
  1. After standing in the correct position, add the appropriate lead Pokémon to the first slot of your party, then open the pause menu and connect to owoow. This will freeze the RNG state and prevent unwanted advances.

  2. Calibrate your NPC count, then close the MenuCloseTimeline sub-window. Verify that your NPC count has been entered into the main window and keep the Consider Menu Close? checkbox active.

  3. Search for a target frame, if you do not find a result consider checking the Holding Direction? checkbox, this may help with finding a target that is hittable using menu close prediction.

  4. If you still do not see any target frames in the search results with both checkboxes active, consider restarting the game to reset your seed, increasing your search range, or applying less strict search filters.

  5. Once you have found a target frame that can be reached using menu close prediction, take note of your positioning (alternatively, do not move), If you change location or move the character you will need to re-calibrate your NPC count again.

The calibration checkboxes (Consider Menu Close? / Holding Direction?) will account for the advances consumed when closing the pause menu, the number of advances will vary depending on whether you are holding a direction on your control stick when unpausing. The search result table will take these advances into account and will now indicate the exact frame on which to close the pause menu and spawn your target Pokémon.

Step-by-Step
  1. Advance toward your target using any of the methods listed on this page. Consider using wet weather or date skipping to advance large distances.

  2. Stop advancing once you are a few thousand advances before your target, then reposition yourself outside the spawn point if you have moved.

  3. Ensure that the Consider Menu Close? checkbox is checked in the main window, and confirm that your NPC count is calibrated correctly.

  4. Use animations or menu close to advance the RNG state until you are approximately 100 advances away from your target, then pause the game.

  5. Reconnect to owoow and search for your target again. From this point, use animations to reach the exact target frame, then return to the main pause menu.

  6. Unpause the game. If Holding Direction? is checked, hold a direction on the control stick when unpausing; otherwise, do not touch the control stick when unpausing.

  7. Quickly run toward the target Pokémon to spawn it in the overworld. Encounter the Pokémon and verify that your result matches what is expected.
