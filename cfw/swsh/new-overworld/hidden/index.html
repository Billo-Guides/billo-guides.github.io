Overworld Hidden Encounters

Worked examples for manipulating Hidden encounters in any weather condition.

HIDDEN RNG SHOULD ONLY BE USED IF NO OTHER OPTIONS ARE AVAILABLE FOR YOUR TARGET POKEMON, PICK A DIFFERENT ENCOUNTER TYPE WHERE POSSIBLE. HIDDEN RNG MAY TAKE MULTIPLE ATTEMPTS BEFORE HITTING A RESULT!

The species that appear in Hidden spawners are determined by a value called an "encounter slot", which ranges from 0 to 99. This value dictates which Pokémon from the encounter table will spawn. The available Pokémon in the encounter table remain the same until the weather in the area changes, which occurs at midnight (00:00). Be mindful of the current system time to prevent unexpected spawns due to incorrect weather conditions.

Important - From here, we assume that you are familiar with key concepts described on the main page. If you are unsure about seed monitoring, advancement methods, or NPC calibration, please review the relevant sections before continuing.
Figure 1 - RNG Tool Setup

Getting Started (Tool Setup):
  1. Enter the IP address of your console in the main window and click Connect. This will automatically read your current seed and save file details.

  2. Click Encounter Lookup in the top toolbar to open a sub-window. Enter the desired species name in the search field and select a HIDDEN encounter.

  3. Close the Encounter Lookup window, then input the relevant Location, Weather, and Species information into the Encounter Settings section.

  4. Select your Lead Ability & Max Step, it is recommended to use an ability that boosts the rate of triggering a hidden encounter and a Max Step count of 1.

  5. Adjust your search range using the + field in Encounter Settings, then configure additional search filters such as Shiny, Marks, IVs, or Height.

Figure 2 - Game Setup

Game Setup:
  1. Remove any Pokémon stored in the {% include tooltips/daycare.html %} and then fly to the location where your target Pokémon can be found.

  2. Find the largest patch of grass in the area, and position yourself in a corner where possible. Make sure you are facing the grass and not a wall.

  3. If wild Pokémon are attacking you, consider changing the weather and repositioning yourself until you find areas with fewer aggressive spawns.

  4. Set the required weather condition by changing the in-game date. The spawned Pokémon will remain the same until the weather changes or they despawn.

  5. If you are riding your bike, dismount it. Hidden encounters are less likely to trigger while riding a bike, even with an optimal lead ability.

  6. Add your desired lead Pokémon to your party and save the game. For more information about lead abilities and their use cases, see below.

In most cases, you will encounter extra wild Pokémon, which can cause unwanted advances when trying to hit a target frame. Managing this issue depends on the chosen weather condition, and the solution will be explained as you progress through this guide.
