Step-by-Step:
  1. Advance toward your target using a method that does not cause the player to move. The best method for advancing the RNG state will vary depending on the size of your cluster:

    • If your target is not in a cluster, it is recommended to use wet weather menuing. This method offers greater consistency for hitting single-frame targets.

    • If your target is in a cluster of three or more, you can safely use overworld advancement methods such as wet weather or date skipping.

  2. Stop advancing when you are a few thousand frames away from your target. If you've moved, reposition yourself optimally inside a grass patch (see above).

  3. Verify your current NPC count and ensure that the Consider Menu Close? and Holding Direction? checkboxes are enabled in the main window.

  4. Reconnect to owoow and search for your target again. From this point, use animations to reach your exact target frame, then back out to the main pause menu.

  5. Unpause the game while holding a direction on your control stick. This will make the player take steps in the grass, triggering the Hidden encounter.

  6. Quickly initiate the encounter before it despawns and verify the result matches expectations, spawned Hidden encounters disappear within a few seconds.

Important - Hidden encounter RNG manipulation may be inconsistent depending on your setup, so it may take multiple attempts to achieve a result. If you are still missing targets after 2-3 attempts, consider changing location or picking a different encounter type.
