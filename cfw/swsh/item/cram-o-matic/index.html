Cram-o-Matic RNG (CFW)

A guide for manipulating the Cram-o-Matic machine to obtain rare Poke Balls and other goodies.

The Cram-o-Matic machine is located inside the Dojo on the Isle of Armor, and can be unlocked through story progression. Once you have unlocked access to the machine, you can "feed" it items from your bag to generate items, such as the rare Apricorn, Safari & Sport Balls or Ribbon & Star sweets for Alcremie. Rare items will be the main focus of this guide as most other items can be obtained through different, more accessible means.

In-game requirements:
  • Access to the Sword/Shield Isle of Armor DLC.
  • Access to the Cram-o-Matic machine.
  • Plenty of resources to feed to the machine.

Rare Balls - Have plenty of Apricorns available, these can be found on berry trees in the Isle of Armor. Any combination of apricorns can be fed to the Cram-o-Matic to generate any type of Poke Ball in the table.

Rare Sweets - Have plenty of common sweets and Whipped Dream. Common sweets can be found daily inside the Battle Cafe, and Whipped Dream can be found in the Hammerlocke Hills BP shop for 10 Battle Points.
