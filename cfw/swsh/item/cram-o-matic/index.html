Cram-o-matic RNG (CFW)

A guide for manipulating the Cram-o-matic to obtain rare Poke Balls and other goodies.

The Cram-o-matic is located inside the Dojo on the Isle of Armor, and can be unlocked through story progression. Once you have unlocked access to the machine, you can "feed" it items from your bag to generate items, such as the rare Apricorn, Safari & Sport Balls or Ribbon & Star sweets for Alcremie. Rare items will be the main focus of this guide as most other items can be obtained through different, more accessible means.

In-game requirements:
  • Access to the Sword/Shield Isle of Armor DLC.
  • Access to the Cram-o-matic machine.
  • Plenty of resources to feed to the machine (see table).
Item Required Recipe
Heavy Ball At least 1 Black Apricorn, followed by 3 Apricorns of any color.
Lure Ball At least 1 Blue Apricorn, followed by 3 Apricorns of any color.
Friend Ball At least 1 Green Apricorn, followed by 3 Apricorns of any color.
Love Ball At least 1 Pink Apricorn, followed by 3 Apricorns of any color.
Level Ball At least 1 Red Apricorn, followed by 3 Apricorns of any color.
Fast Ball At least 1 White Apricorn, followed by 3 Apricorns of any color.
Moon Ball At least 1 Yellow Apricorn, followed by 3 Apricorns of any color.
Safari Ball Any mixture of 4 different Apricorns.
Sport Ball 4 of the same type of Apricorn.
Ribbon & Star Sweet

Recipe 1 - 4 common Alcremie sweets (Strawberry, Love, Berry, Clover, Flower).

Recipe 2 - 3 common Alcremie sweets followed by 1 Whipped Dream.

Recipe 3 - 2 common Alcremie sweets followed by 2 Whipped Dream.

Recipe 4 - 1 Whipped Dream followed by 3 Weakness Policy (BP Items).
Information - Apricorns can be found randomly in berry trees scattered around the Isle of Armor, common Alcremie sweets can be found inside the Battle Cafes as daily rewards, Whipped Dream can be purchased in the Hammerlocke Hills BP shop for 10 BP, and Weakness Policy can be purchased in the Battle Tower for 20 BP.
