Watt Trader RNG (CFW)

A guide for manipulating the daily highlight item from the Watt Trader NPC.

The Watt Trader NPC is located at the bottom of Snowslide Slope in the Crown Tundra. Each day, this NPC has a different highlighted item, some of which are the rare Dream/Beast Balls. This guide will focus on manipulating his daily highlight item for a desirable result.

Important - From here, we assume that you are familiar with key concepts described on the main page. If you are unsure about seed monitoring, advancement methods, or NPC calibration, please review the relevant sections before continuing.
In-game Requirements
  • Access to the Crown Tundra DLC.
  • Plenty of Watts to spend on the highlight item (each rare Pokéball costs around 40,000 Watts).
  • Interacted with the NPC at least once to skip the introduction and unlock the correct fly point.
Need an easy method to farm watts? Visit the Digging Pa guide!

Getting Started (Game Setup)
  1. Head to the above location in the Crown Tundra, and interact with the Watt Trader at least once to gain access to the fly point if you do not already have access to it.

  2. Perform a date advance to refresh the daily highlight item. This can be done using any date skipping method.

  3. Fly into the area, this will provide optimal positioning and refresh the highlight item if you have date skipped.

  4. Knock out the spawned Static Pokémon that is behind you, this will cause unwanted advances if left standing.

  5. Re-position yourself similarly to Figure 1, ensure that you have a clear, straight line between the player and the Watt Trader.

  6. Check your current weather condition by opening the map, and calibrate your NPC count accordingly (do not use Rain/Thunderstorm for obvious reasons).

  7. Save the game.

Figure 2 - RNG Tool Setup

Getting Started (Tool Setup)
  1. Enter the IP address of your console in the main window and click Connect. This will automatically read your current seed and save file details.

  2. At the top of the main owoow window, select Watt Trader to open the Watt Trader generator in a sub-window.

  3. Enter your search filters, then enter the amount of advances you are willing to search through into the + field, usually you will find a result that produces a Dream/Beast Ball in less than x advances.

  4. Set the correct weather condition, this is important for accurate search results then using menu close prediction as the Watt Trader is located outside.

  5. Activate the Consider Menu Close? checkbox, then enter the NPC count that was calibrated earlier. You can now begin searching for targets.

If you do not find a desirable result you will need to increase your search range, reset the game and follow the above steps again, or apply less strict search filters.


Important - It is important to understand how NPC activation works. When standing next to an NPC it takes them a moment to notice you and become "activated", which will cause the speed of advances to increase. If you interact with the NPC before they notice you, you can avoid "activating" them. The goal is to avoid activating NPCs when attempting to hit your target frame.

Advancing to the Target

In this example, a frame that produces a Beast Ball can be seen in a {% include tooltips/clusters.html %} between advances x-y, these are the advances that we will aim to close the pause menu on, then quickly interact with the Watt Trader NPC using the method shown in Figure 4. It is only important to interact with the NPC on this frame to lock our daily highlight item in place.

The first two days of daily highlights will be locked in after speaking to the Watt Trader NPC, which means our target item will be available on the third day. To get to our desired highlight item we will need to perform 2 date skips and interact with the Watt Trader twice after we have done the RNG.

Step-by-Step
  1. Advance toward your target using any of the methods listed on this page.

    • Consider using wet weather or date skipping to advance large distances (for targets > 100,000 advances, it is unlikely you will need to use this).

    • Consider using NPC activation or menu close prediction to advance shorter distances (for targets < 100,000 advances, most targets will be less than this).

  2. Stop advancing once you are ~1000 advances before your target, then reposition yourself optimally as shown in Figure 1 if you have moved.

  3. Ensure that the Consider Menu Close? checkbox is checked in the Watt Trader sub-window, and confirm that your NPC count is calibrated correctly.

  4. Click Update Seeds on the main window of owoow, then re-open the Watt Trader sub-window and search for your target again.

  5. From this point, use animations to reach the exact target frame, then return to the main pause menu.

  6. Unpause the game. If Holding Direction? is checked, hold a direction on the control stick when unpausing; otherwise, do not touch the control stick when unpausing.

  7. Move towards the Watt Trader NPC quickly using the method shown in Figure 4, interact with them to lock your highlight item in place, ready to pick up on the third day. Now click Today's Highlight to lock in the expected result.
Accessing Our Daily Highlight
  1. Exit out of the Watt Trader dialogue.

  2. Perform a date skip.

  3. Interact with the Watt Trader, putting us on "day 2".

  4. Exit out of the Watt Trader dialogue.

  5. Perform another date skip

  6. Interact with the Watt Trader again, putting us on "day 3".

  7. Check Today's Highlight, this should be your desired item.

In Figure 6 we closed the pause menu on the desired target frame and quickly interacted with the Watt Trader before they became active, now we need to select Today's Highlight to lock in our expected reward, then date skip using the above method to have him produce our desired item.

After date skipping and interacting with the Watt Trader enough times using the method described above, we can see that Today's Highlight features out desired item!
