Digging Pa RNG (CFW)

A guide for manipulating the Digging Pa to farm a large number of Watts.

The Digging Pa NPC is located on the north side of the Isle of Armor. Interacting with him gives you the option to trade Armorite Ore in return for attempts at his minigame, where you can earn a large amount of Watts.

Important - From here, we assume that you are familiar with key concepts described on the main page. If you are unsure about topics like seed monitoring, advancement methods, or NPC calibration, please review the relevant sections before continuing.
In-game Requirements
  • Access to the Isle of Armor DLC.
  • Armorite Ore to give to the NPC.
  • Interacted with the NPC at least once to skip the introduction.

Getting Started (Game Setup)
  1. Head to the above location in the Isle of Armor, and interact with the Digging Pa at least once to gain access to the fly point.

  2. Fly into the area (this will priovide optimal positioning), and knock out the spawned static Pokemon that is behind you.

  3. Re-position yourself similarly to Figure 1, ensure that you have a clear, straight line between the player and the Digging Pa.

  4. Check your current weather condition by opening the map, and calibrate your NPC count accordingly (do not use Rain/Thunderstorm for obvious reasons).

  5. Save the game.

The NPC count in the Isle of Armor is variable, you will need to re-calibrate your NPC count if the date has naturally advanced or you have reloaded the area after a date advance.


Figure 2 - RNG Tool Setup

Getting Started (Tool Setup)
  1. Enter the IP address of your console in the main window and click Connect. This will automatically read your current seed and save file details.

  2. At the top of the main owoow window, select Digging Pa to open the Digging Pa generator in a sub-window.

  3. Enter the amount of advances you are willing to search through into the + field, usually you will find a result that produces 500,000 Watts in less than 10,000 advances.

  4. Set the correct weather condition, this is important for accurate search results then using menu close prediction, as the Digging Pa is located outside.

  5. Activate the Consider Menu Close? checkbox, then enter the NPC count that was calibrated earlier. You can now begin searching for targets.

If you do not find a desirable result you will need to increase your search range, reset the game and follow the above steps again, or apply less strict search filters.


Important - It is important to understand how NPC activation works. When standing next to an NPC it takes them a moment to notice you and become "activated", which will cause the speed of advances to increase. If you interact with the NPC before they notice you, you can avoid "activating" them. The goal is to avoid activating NPCs when attempting to hit your target frame.

Advancing to the Target

In this example, a frame that produces a 520,000~ Watts can be seen in a {% include tooltips/clusters.html %} between advances 982-984, these are the advances that we will aim to close the pause menu on, then quickly interact with the Digging Pa NPC using the method shown in Figure 4. It is only important to interact with the NPC on this frame to lock our Watts in place.

Step-by-Step
  1. Advance toward your target using any of the methods listed on this page.

    • Consider using wet weather or date skipping to advance large distances (for targets > 100,000 advances, it is unlikely you will need to use this).

    • Consider using NPC activation or menu close prediction to advance shorter distances (for targets < 100,000 advances, most targets will be less than this).

  2. Stop advancing once you are ~1000 advances before your target, then reposition yourself optimally as shown in Figure 1 if you have moved.

  3. Ensure that the Consider Menu Close? checkbox is checked in the Digging Pa sub-window, and confirm that your NPC count is calibrated correctly.

  4. Click Update Seeds on the main window of owoow, then re-open the Digging Pa sub-window and search for your target again.

  5. From this point, use animations to reach the exact target frame, then return to the main pause menu.

  6. Unpause the game. If Holding Direction? is checked, hold a direction on the control stick when unpausing; otherwise, do not touch the control stick when unpausing.

  7. Move towards the Digging Pa NPC quickly using the method shown in Figure 4, interact with them to lock your Watt result in place, then click Dig me up some Watts to produce the expected result.
