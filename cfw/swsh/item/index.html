Item RNG Guide (CFW)

This portion of the guide will cover the process of RNG abusing the Cram-o-matic and the PC lottery function in Sword/Shield using the SWSH Overworld RNG Generator by LegoFigure11. Item RNG abuse is best utilized to help players obtain rare/valuable items such as rare Pokeballs!

This guide assumes you are using a CFW Switch console running Atmosphere.

Click here if you are using a unmodified Switch console!

Getting Started:

You will need:
  • SWSH Overworld RNG Generator - Latest release
  • .NET 6.0 - Generator dependency
  • sys-usb-botbase - Latest release
  • ldn-mitm - Latest release
Installation Instructions (Click to expand!)
  1. Download and extract all the contents from the SWSH_OWRNG_Generator_GUI.zip into an empty folder.
  2. Download and extract all the contents from the sys-usb-botbase & ldn-mitm downloads into an empty folder.
  3. Place the atmosphere folder(s) from these downloads onto the root of your Switch SD card.
  4. Check the /atmosphere/contents directory of your SD card for two folders named 430000000000000B & 4200000000000010.
  5. Reboot your Switch console and launch CFW.

In-game setup:
  • Have the Rotom PC lottery function & Cram-o-matic available for use.
  • If you plan to RNG abuse the Cram-o-matic, have at least 4 apricorns to use in the machine.
  • Stand next to the machine you want to work on.
  • Pause the game using "X", click Connect on the main window of the generator.

Important Information

Advancing the RNG state:

The RNG state will advance naturally depending on your location, and the amount of NPCs that are present. Since the Cram-o-matic and Rotom PC are located in-doors, we do not need to worry about other variables that would advance the RNG state, like weather.

Multiple in-game actions can also be used to advance the RNG state with varying influence, the most useful actions can be found in the table directly below.

Type of action Description of action Adv. influence
Player Movement Moving the player using the control stick, then letting the control stick fall back into it's resting state. This Includes whistle and bike bell.

 1-3 Advancements per input.
Menu Close Opening and closing the pause menu using the X button.

 Varying depending on location and environment, predictable (explained below).
NPC Activation Standing near an NPC to make them notice the player. Moving away from an NPC will cause them to "deactivate". Useful for covering short distances quickly.

 ~2500 advancements per minute.
Animations/Motions Having your Pokémon animate on the summary screen or party menu by clicking in the L3 button, or by using the Skip feature.

 +1 advancement per animation on the individual Pokémon summary screen.

+1 advancement per button input on the party summary screen.

Menu Close Prediction Overview
The Menu Close Advances Viewer can be used to predict the amount of advancements that will pass from closing the pause menu. Closing the pause menu also has an added effect of freezing the RNG state in the area for a a few seconds, this makes it a great option for closing in on any target frames as it provides a lot more consistency in doing so.

For Loto-ID and the Cram-o-Matic he NPC Counts are likely as follows:
Wedgehurst Center = 5
Dojo (Completed IoA Story) = 21

By knowing how many advancements will occur from closing the pause menu, and having a window of silence to interact with the machine, we can near guarantee to hit any potential target frames.

When using the Consider menu close feature, the output results are of the frame that you need to close the menu on, NOT the frame that you need to start the interaction on.

How to calculate the number of NPCs in an area:
  1. Press the X button on your controller to open the pause menu, then disconnect and reconnect to the main window. This will ensure the number inside the Curr. Adv remains at 0.

  2. Click the Menu Close Advances Viewer button at the top of the main window to open a smaller window. Your seeds should be visible in the correct fields.

  3. Close the pause menu then quickly re-open it, take note of the number of advancements that have occured from doing so.

  4. Enter your number of advancements into the Init Adv. field. If the above steps were closely followed, this number should be 0.

  5. Type 0 into the NPCs field and search. Gradually increase this number by 1 until you find a match. Repeat this step a few times to ensure the advancement predictions are accurate.

Cram-o-matic RNG abuse

The goal is to interact with the Cram-o-matic on the calculated target frame given to us by the Cram-o-matic window of the generator. We will be using the advancement methods listed in the table above to hit the target frame.

In this example we will be aiming for a 5 Level Balls. You can be more specific with the filters if you wish, such as filtering for a specific rare items.

Game Setup
We are now stood directly next to the Cram-o-matic and have the console connected to the generator.

Once the player interacts with the Cram-o-matic, the RNG state will freeze due to being on a menu screen. This is why it is only important to interact with the Cram-o-matic on the correct frame.

Once the Cram-o-matic tab is filled in correctly, you can begin looking for target frames by clicking the search button.

If your results are not desirable, repeat the following:

  1. Disconnect your Switch from the tool.
  2. Restart your game and enter the overworld.
  3. Pause the game, then reconnect your Switch to the tool.
  4. Search again, do this until you have desirable results.

Hitting your target frame
Step-by-step tutorial:

  1. Advance towards your target frame using any of the methods listed inside the table above.

  2. Once you are within a few hundred frames of your target, stand within distance of the Cram-o-matic so that you can immediately interact with it in a single A press.

  3. Pause the game using the X button, then disconnect and reconnect to the main window, then search for your target again in the Cram-o-matic window.

    4. Tip: From this point it's recommended to only advance using menu close & animations as they only advance in small increments, making them easy to keep track of.

  4. Open the Cram-o-matic window, enter your target frame into the + field and the amount of NPCs into the NPCs field, then click search.

    5. Tip: If you are unable to find your target frame in the search results, this means the target cannot be hit based on your seed. Use animations to advance the RNG state slightly, then close and re-open the Cram-o-matic window and search for your target frame again. Repeat this process until your target frame is visible.

  5. Open and close the pause menu until closing the menu a single time lands onto your target frame, according to the Loto-ID window prediction. Pay attention to the Curr. Adv field of the main window to avoid overshooting your target.

  6. Close the pause menu and quickly interact with the Cram-o-matic, if you believe you hit your target frame, feed it the appropriate apricorns to claim your reward.

    7. You can be creative with the methods you use to advance the RNG state. Menu close prediction is not necessary but will make for a much easier time hitting target frames and is extremely helpful in areas of the game where the RNG advances at a quick pace.
