Item RNG (CFW)

This portion of the guide will cover the process of RNG abusing the Cram-o-matic and the PC lottery function in Sword/Shield using the SWSH Overworld RNG Generator by LegoFigure11. Item RNG abuse is best utilized to help players obtain rare/valuable items such as rare Pokeballs!

This guide assumes you are using a CFW Switch console running Atmosphere.

Click here if you are using a unmodified Switch console!

Getting Started:

You will need:
  • SWSH Overworld RNG Generator - Latest release
  • sys-usb-botbase - Latest release
  • ldn-mitm - Latest release
Installation Instructions:
  1. Download and extract all the contents from the SWSH_OWRNG_Generator_GUI.zip into an empty folder.
  2. Download and extract all the contents from the sys-usb-botbase and ldn-mitm downloads into an empty folder.
  3. Place the atmosphere folder(s) from these downloads onto the root of your Switch SD card and reboot the console.
  4. Check the /atmosphere/contents directory of your SD card for two folders named 430000000000000B & 4200000000000010.
  5. Reboot your Switch console and launch CFW.
In-game setup:
  • Have the Rotom PC lottery function & Cram-o-matic available for use.
  • If you plan to RNG abuse the Cram-o-matic, have at least 4 apricorns to use in the machine.
  • Stand next to the machine you want to work on.
  • Pause the game using "X", click Connect on the main window of the generator.

Advancing the RNG state:

Multiple in-game actions can be used to advance the RNG state with varying influence, these include:
  1. Player movement - Each time the player stops moving the control stick the RNG state will advance a small amount - Roughly 1-3 advancements & a spammable action.

  2. Menu close - Opening and closing the pause menu will advance the RNG state a small amount depending on your location and amount of NPCs present. - Predictable advancements & a spammable action.

  3. Animations - You can make your Pokemon animate on the party summary screen by pressing in the left stick. - 1 advancment per "click" & a spammable action.

  4. Active NPCs - Standing near an NPC will "activate" them, this will advance the RNG state rapidly. - Area specific advancements, good for covering large amounts of advancements quickly.

Menu Close Prediction:

We can accurately predict the number of advancements that will pass when we close the pause menu. It is based on the number of NPCs in the area and if you were holding a direction on the control stick at the time of unpause. For Loto-ID and Cram-o-matic, the NPC Counts are likely as follows:

Wedgehurst Center: 5
Dojo (Completed IoA Story): 21

If you do not get the correct results, or are attempting to RNG something outside of the above areas, you will need to Calculate your NPC Count.

This can be done using the Menu Close Advances Viewer as follows:
  1. Count the number of NPCs in the area you're in manually. This can also include Pokemon and models outside of render distance so may not be completely accurate, this simply provides a good starting point.
  2. Get into position and press "X" to open the menu.
  3. Connect to the generator. The number in the Curr Adv. field should be 0.
  4. Click the Menu Close Advances Viewer button at the top of the generator to open a smaller window. Your seeds should be visible in the correct fields.
  5. Take the number of NPCs you counted earlier and put it into the NPCs field.
  6. Enter the search range (a few hundred should be enough!) into the + field directly below and search, you should then close and reopen the menu to make sure the predicted advances match what you actually get.
  7. If the values do not match, tweak the number in the NPCs field (decrease the value if the number of advances you got was lower than expected, increase if it was higher) until it matches. Now do a few more menu advances then re-identify your current seed to make sure you stay on track with the generated "timeline".
Closing the pause menu has an added effect of freezing noise in the area for a short time period, we will use this window of silence in tandem with menu close prediction to near guarantee that we interact with the machine on the correct frame.

Menu close prediction is not a requirement to perform item RNG abuse, this is the recommended method to use as it allows you to precisely advance all the way up to your target using animations instead of relying on frame clusters or luck. This comes with the side effect of potentially finding fewer results but provides a lot more consistency.

With all the required reading out of the way, let's get into it!


Cram-o-matic RNG abuse (CFW)

Time to get to work!

The goal is to interact with the Cram-o-matic on the calculated target frame given to us by the Cram-o-matic tab of the generator. We will be using the advancement methods listed at the top of the page and using menu close prediction to hit the target frame.

If you plan to use the DaySkip feature you should take note of how many frames advance per day skipped to ensure that you don't overshoot your target.

In this example we will be aiming for a 5 Level Balls. You can be more specific with the filters if you wish, such as filtering for a specific rare items.

Once the Cram-o-matic tab is filled in correctly, you can begin looking for target frames by clicking the search button.

If your results are not desirable, repeat the following:
  1. Disconnect your Switch from the generator.
  2. Restart your game and enter the overworld.
  3. Enter the pause or Home menu and reconnect your Switch to the generator.
  4. Search again, do this until you have desirable results.
When using the Consider menu close feature, the output results are of the frame that you need to close the menu on, NOT the frame that you need to start the interaction on.

After closing the pause menu on the correct target frame you should be able to quickly interact with the Cram-o-matic machine to claim your reward.

Advancing towards the target:
The generator displays the target on frame 795, we do not need to account for any delay. This means we will aim to interact with the Cram-o-matic on frame 795.

There are 21 NPCs in the area, we need this information to accurately predict the amount of advancements that will occur from closing the pause menu.

Step by step:
  1. Advance towards the target by standing near NPCs in the dojo and using player movement.

    2. If your target is a large number of frames away consider using the day skipping method described at the top of this page.

  2. Once you are close to the target (within ~500 frames), stand near to the Cram-o-matic.

    3. From this point it's recommended to only advance using menu close and animations as they only advance in small increments, making them easy to keep track of.

  3. Open the Cram-o-matic window of the generator, enter the amount of NPCs you have in the dojo into the field, then search for your target again.

    4. The Jump column displays the amount of advancements that will pass from closing the menu when considering menu close prediction.

  4. Advance closer to your target frame (within ~100 frames this time) and open the pause menu again, open the Pokémon summary page, update your current seed then search in the Cram-o-matic window once more.

    5. Make sure you are facing the Cram-o-matic at this point.

    From this point it's recommended to only advance using animations from the party menu, this provide a stable +1 advancement per input.

  5. Open the party menu and advance to your target frame.

  6. Close the pause menu and quickly interact with the Cram-o-matic. If successful, you will have hit your target frame! Feed it 4 apricorns and claim your reward.

You can be creative with the methods you use to advance the RNG state.

Player movement will advance the RNG state 1-2 frames with each new stick input, allowing for more precise advancements during the window of silence, making it much easier to hit a target frame if we undershoot for any reason.
