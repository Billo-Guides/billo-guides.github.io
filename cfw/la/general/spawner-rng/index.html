Spawner RNG Legendary Abuse

This page will provide in-depth information about how to RNG abuse Legendary Pokemon in Legends: Arceus that are tied to what is known as Spawners. We will be using RNG automation programs written by Zyro670, as mentioned on the main page.

From here it is assumed that you have already followed all of the setup instructions for Spawner RNG abuse, found on the introductory page of this guide.

About Spawner RNG:

Pokemon that are tied to spawners have their seed generated when you enter the map or cave that they are located in. The seed remains the same until you defeat the Pokemon and have it respawn, this will be a consistent +1 advancement to the Spawner seed.

Leaving the area and heading back to Jubilife HQ will refresh the spawner seed of every Pokemon on the map, including Legendary Pokemon. We can use Zyro670's Sysbot to automate the seed checking process.

It is recommended to have a strong Pokemon, lots of healing items (e.g Elixirs, Potions, Revives) and lots of throwable items (Ball of Mud, Sticky Glob etc.) before getting started. You will need enough resources to consistently defeat/stun the Legendary Pokemon that you are hunting.


Now we know the basics about Spawner-based encounters, it's time to get to work!

Getting Started (Spawner RNG)

After you have gathered enough resources you will need to configure Sysbot.NET properly for the game you are using and the Pokemon you plan to hunt. In this example we will be hunting for Enamorus with flawless IVs.

Sysbot.NET Setup & Instructions:
Sysbot.NET Config:
  1. Open the Sysbot.NET application once and close it again to generate a config.json file inside the same folder as Sysbot.NET.
  2. Open config.json in a text editor like Notepad, then change the Mode to 3 in order to use the Legends: Arceus bots, then save the changes.
  3. Open Sysbot.NET again, you should now see the Sysbot: Pokemon (LA) in the header, this means the bot has configured for Legends: Arceus successfully.

Next, configure the bot to hunt for your desired Pokemon and set the amount of frames to search through, along with setting your desired StopConditions.

Spawner RNG Setup:
  1. Open the Hub tab of Sysbot.NET, then open the ArceusLA dropdown menu. This is where you will add the relevant information specific to your chosen hunt.
  2. Set the BotType to the Pokemon you are hunting. For Genies use GenieScanner, for Manaphy use ManaphyReset, and for anything else use OtherLegendReset.
  3. Open the SpecialConditions dropdown menu, then select the correct ScanLocation for the Pokemon you are hunting. (Example: for Enamorus, select Crimson Mirelands)
  4. If you have chosen OtherLegendReset, you will also need to select the TypeOfLegend that you are hunting to make sure the bot performs the correct routine.
  5. Set the MaxAdvancesToSearch to an the amount of advancements you are comfortable with doing (50-100 is recommended).
  6. Set RunToProfessor to false so you can take advantage of teleportation when leaving the area to save time.
  7. Scroll up to the top, and open the StopConditions dropdown menu, type in your desired IVs/Nature combination, then restart the bot (close and re-open).

Once you have configured the Hub tab details properly head back over to the Bots tab, then add the ArceusBot using the local IP address of your Switch console.

In-game Setup:

Different routines are used depending on the the Pokemon you are hunting and the area it is located in. This means that the in-game setup will also differ depending on which Pokemon you are hunting for.

For Pokemon located in the overworld, you will need to start from Jubilife, then manually enter the map that contains the Pokemon you wish to hunt.

These are - Tornadus, Thundurus, Landorus, Enamorus, Cresselia, Shaymin & Darkrai.

For Pokemon located inside caves, you will need to save the game stood outside of the cave entry point of the Pokemon you wish to hunt.

These are - Uxie, Mesprit, Azelf, Heatran & Manaphy.

Advancing Towards The Target:

Frame advancement methods will differ depending on if your Pokemon is an overworld spawn or is a cave spawn, this section will cover how to advance frames in both of these scenarios.

Overworld Pokemon & Manaphy:

These include - Tornadus, Thundurus, Landorus, Enamorus, Cresselia, Manaphy, Shaymin & Darkrai.

  1. Knockout the legendary.
  2. Fly to a campsite, then sleep until night, then sleep again until morning.
  3. Fly to a cave and enter, then save and reboot the game.
  4. Fly back to the legendary and wait 1 weather cycle for the legendary to respawn.
  5. Repeat from step 1 until the desired frame has been hit.
  6. Alternatively, if you wish to advance using the bot you can use this method instead.
Cave Pokemon (Not Manaphy):

These include - Uxie, Mesprit, Azelf & Heatran.

  1. Knockout the legendary.
  2. Click through the menus until you regain control of your character.
  3. Repeat from step 1 until the desired frame has been hit.

You can use PokeViewer.NET to read the current spawned Legendary Pokemon, then compare it to the Sysbot logs output to keep track of your current frame if you get lost along the way.
