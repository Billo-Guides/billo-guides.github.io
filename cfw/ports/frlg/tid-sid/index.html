TID/SID Manipulation (CFW)

A guide for RNG manipulating your TID/SID in the Switch ports of FireRed & LeafGreen.
Introduction / Preface

Finding yourself on this page means you are interested in manipulating your TID/SID combination. As mentioned on the main page, TID/SID manipulation allows you to control which PIDs generate shiny Pokémon, or to target specific TID/SID values for aesthetic or personal preference. This section of the guide covers the full process of obtaining a desired TID and RNG manipulating an SID to make a specific PID shiny.

What you will do in this section of the guide will depend on your goal. In most cases you will want to either:

  • Obtain a specific TID/SID combination to make a specific spread/PID shiny.
  • Obtain a specific TID for the sake of aesthetics or personal preference.

Consider your goals. If you have a specific spread in mind that you would like to generate as a shiny, move onto Figure 1. Otherwise you can move directly to Figure 2 for TID botting setup instructions.


Figure 1 - SID RNG Initial Setup

SID RNG Initial Setup

You will need to decide on the Spread/PID you would like to make shiny. The Tools tab of GRASS provides an IVs to PID tool that can be used to search for specific PID values based on entered IVs.

Step-by-step
  1. Launch GRASS, then open the Tools tab.
  2. Type your desired IVs into the IVs to PID section.
  3. Select a method, in most cases you should target Method 1.
    • Method 1 is the most common method, controlling the method is considered an advanced technique and should only be done once you gain enough experience.
  4. Select a Nature if you wish to target a specific IV/Nature combination.
  5. Click Generate, you should now see a list of spreads in the results table.
    • Not all spreads are possible, for example, a Timid 31/0/31/31/31/31 spread does not exist at all regardless of the Method.
    • If you get no search results after clicking Generate, it means that no spreads match your search criteria.
    • Adjust your search filters accordingly by either loosening the IV or Nature filter, e.g, try filtering for any nature and see if there are any you like.
  6. Pick a spread from the results table, then note down the PID somewhere (you will need this later).

Figure 2 - TID Botting Initial Setup

TID Botting Initial Setup

GRASS can automate the process of finding a desirable TID. Before doing anything else you will need to create a list of acceptable TIDs by doing the following:

Step-by-step
  1. Launch GRASS, then open the TID/SID tab.
  2. Click the Edit TID List button to open a sub-window.
  3. Enter your desired TID into the ID: field, then click Add.
  4. Repeat the previous step until you have a list of acceptable TIDs.
  5. Close the sub-window after entering all of your desired TIDs.

If you have a long list of TIDs you would accept you can consider opening tid-list.json in a text editor like notepad and entering your TIDs directly into the list. This may cause issues if you do not know what you are doing but is a more time efficient approach.
