TID/SID Manipulation (CFW)

A guide for RNG manipulating your TID/SID in the Switch ports of FireRed & LeafGreen.
Introduction / Preface

If you're on this page, you are looking to manipulate your TID, SID, or both! As explained on the main page, this process lets you control which PIDs become shiny or aim for specific TID combinations for aesthetic reasons. This section covers both obtaining a desired TID and manipulating your SID so a chosen PID becomes shiny. Your approach depends on your goal, in most cases you will either:

  • Target a specific TID purely for aesthetics or personal preference.
  • Target a random TID/SID combination to make a desired spread or PID shiny.
  • Target a specific TID for aesthetics AND manipulate your SID to make a desired spread or PID shiny.

Select your desired use case from the list below to get started. Be aware that searching for a specific TID and an SID to make a specific spread shiny may take a while as it can take some time to find a matching TID and still relies on a perfectly timed inputs to hit the desired SID.
