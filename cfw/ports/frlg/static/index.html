Static Gift / Encounter RNG (CFW)

A guide for RNG manipulating Pokémon from static gifts or encounters in the Switch ports of FireRed & LeafGreen.
What are "Statics"?

Static gifts/encounters are Pokémon that are in a fixed location and are generated with an A button press, examples of static gifts/encounters include:

  • Your starter Pokémon
  • Gift Pokémon from other NPCs
  • Pokémon from the Celadon City Game Corner
  • In-game trades
  • Articuno, Zapdos, Moltres
  • Entei, Raikou, Suicune
  • Ho-Oh, Lugia, Deoxys
  • Mewtwo
  • Anything else not included in this list that is generated by pressing the A button.

If your Pokémon is generated by walking randomly by taking steps in grass/caves/water (or using Sweet Scent), you are in the wrong place. Follow the Wild Encounter section of the guide instead.
