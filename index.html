Billo's RNG Guides

About this project...

The goal of this project is to provide in-depth guides for different types of RNG abuse in Pokemon games. All guides will be written to the best extent of my knowledge and will be quality checked before publishing for public consumption.

If you believe that some aspect of the guide could be improved or better written, the best way to leave feedback is by contacting me on Discord (see the bottom of this page for contact details).

Currently, this page supports guides for:

  • SCARLET/VIOLET - RaidCrawler Guide (CFW)
  • SWORD/SHIELD - Overworld/Wild RNG (Retail & CFW)
  • SWORD/SHIELD - Item RNG (Retail & CFW)
  • Legends: Arceus - Spawner Legendary RNG (Retail & CFW)
  • Let's Go Pikachu/Eevee - Overworld Legendary RNG (CFW)

You can use the navigation bar at the top of this page to jump to your page of interest.
